OUR AIM

We aim to ensuring the smooth physical, mental and emotionally development in children and acquisition of positive habits. And prepare children for primary education, creating a common environment of education and development for children coming from disadvantaged families and environments. The main aim of kindergarden, in our opinion is to help a child to develop to their fullest potential and prepare them for their next chapter in life at school.
